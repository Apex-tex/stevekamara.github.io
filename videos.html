VIDEO PRESENTATIONS

Invention/Innovation Video

Abstract English

Blockchain technology represents a groundbreaking development in the field of Information Technology, characterized by its decentralized, secure, and transparent nature. This presentation delves into the debate of whether blockchain should be viewed as an invention or an innovation. By exploring its technical foundations, applications across various industries, and its ongoing evolution, we will uncover the transformative potential of blockchain technology. Join us to understand how blockchain is reshaping the digital landscape and its future implications.

Abstract Kiswahili

Teknolojia ya blockchain inawakilisha maendeleo makubwa katika uwanja wa Teknolojia ya Habari, inayojulikana kwa asili yake ya ugatuzi, usalama, na uwazi. Mwasilisho huu unachunguza mjadala wa ikiwa blockchain inapaswa kuonekana kama uvumbuzi au ubunifu. Kwa kuchunguza misingi yake ya kiufundi, matumizi yake katika tasnia mbalimbali, na mageuzi yake yanayoendelea, tutaonyesha uwezo wa kubadilisha wa teknolojia ya blockchain. Jiunge nasi ili kuelewa jinsi blockchain inavyobadilisha mazingira ya kidijitali na athari zake za baadaye.

Abstract Kikuyu

Teknologī ya block chain yonanītie maendeleo manene harī teknologī ya mohoro na urangīri wa mītambo ya ucenjania wa beca. Block chain yonanītie atī īna ūhoti wa kūmenyerera mītambo na kūgiria imaramari gūtunya andū. Gīthomo gīkī ndīgīcokanīrīirie nīguo kīonanie ūrīa teknologī ya block chain īteithītie

African Culture Video

Abstract English

African storytelling is a rich tradition that has preserved history, taught moral lessons, and united communities for generations. This presentation explores the profound influence of African storytelling on modern Information Technology. By examining traditional narrative structures, data representation, and practical applications, we will uncover how these age-old techniques are shaping contemporary software development, UX design, and AI technologies. Join us to understand the cultural significance and future potential of integrating African storytelling with modern IT.

Abstract Kiswahili

Hadithi za Kiafrika ni utamaduni tajiri ambao umehifadhi historia, kufundisha maadili, na kuunganisha jamii kwa vizazi vingi. Mwasilisho huu unachunguza ushawishi mkubwa wa hadithi za Kiafrika kwenye Teknolojia ya Habari ya kisasa. Kwa kuchunguza miundo ya hadithi za jadi, uwakilishi wa data, na matumizi ya vitendo, tutaonyesha jinsi mbinu hizi za zamani zinavyounda maendeleo ya programu za kisasa, muundo wa UX, na teknolojia za AI. Jiunge nasi ili kuelewa umuhimu wa kitamaduni na uwezo wa baadaye wa kuunganisha hadithi za Kiafrika na IT ya kisasa.

Abstract Kikuyu

Ng’ano nī cia bata mūno harī kūmenyerera ūndūire. Cianatūmīka kaingī gūcokanīrīria andū o ūndū ūmwe na kūmaruta mītugo ya ūndūire wao. Gīthomo gīkī ndīgīcokanīrīirie nīguo kūruta andū ūrīa ng’ano īhūthīkīte na īkarutithania wīra na mītambo ya ūkinyanīria. Thutha wa kūrora gīthomo gīkī wega nitūkuona ūria ng’ano citeithītie guthondeka mītambo ya ūkinyanīria na nenda, o ro ūndū ūmwe na mwerekera wa sekta īno.

Challenges in Modern Society Video

Abstract English

Modern society is grappling with numerous challenges that significantly impact our daily lives. This presentation focuses on three major issues: the hard life caused by the Coronavirus pandemic, global warming, and the decline in social togetherness. We will explore the root causes, current efforts to address these challenges, and propose innovative solutions to overcome them. Join us to understand these pressing issues and how we can collectively work towards a better future.

Abstract Kiswahili

Jamii ya kisasa inakabiliana na changamoto nyingi zinazohatarisha maisha yetu ya kila siku. Mwasilisho huu unalenga masuala matatu makuu: maisha magumu yanayosababishwa na janga la Coronavirus, ongezeko la joto duniani, na kupungua kwa umoja wa kijamii. Tutachunguza chanzo cha matatizo haya, juhudi za sasa za kukabiliana nayo, na kupendekeza suluhisho bunifu za kuyashinda. Jiunge nasi kuelewa masuala haya muhimu na jinsi tunavyoweza kufanya kazi kwa pamoja kuelekea maisha bora ya baadaye.

Abstract Kikuyu

Rūūrīrī rwa rīu nīruonete iremithia ngūrani ta kīng’ūki gīa covid-19 na kwaga mbura kwa imera ithatū. O ro ūndū ūmwe nī tūthūkītie rīera gūkīra gīthimi na nīūndū ūcio tūgatūma ūrugarī wambatīre gūkīra gīthimi. Barafu īthiīte na mbere na kūringūka na nī ūndū ūcio rī nyamu cia gīthaka ta polar bears ciī hatīkainī. Ningī rūūrīrī rwitū nī rwarteanīirie ūrūmwe na ūmundu. Nī twatigire kūgeithanagia na gūteithania. Mūno mūno nī ūndū wa thimū na kūruta wīra mataūniinī. Nītūcokie rūī mūkaro andū aitū.
